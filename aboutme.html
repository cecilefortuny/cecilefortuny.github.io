My name is Cécile, I am 27 and am from France.

I grew up near Lyon, and slowly moved North-East of France, close to Germany and Switzerland. After finishing high school in Mulhouse, Alsace, I moved to Strasbourg to pursue higher education. I first graduated with an Associate Degree in Biotechnology and then completed a Bachelor of Science in Molecular and Cellular Biology at the University Louis Pasteur. I pursued with a Master degree in Drug Science at the Faculty of Pharmacy, where I specialized in pharmacology and drug development.

I immigrated to the US in 2012, eagered to learn english and a different culture than mine. I initially came for 8 months as a Visiting Scholar for my Master's internship and settled in the Bay Area for graduate school. I started my PhD in the Fall 2013.

On my free time, I love traveling and discovering new cultures.
